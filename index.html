About Paws n' Play

Welcome to Paws n’ Play — Vancouver’s first comprehensive dog hub, featuring the city’s first licensed Dog Café alongside full-service grooming, walking, and retail. It’s a vibrant community space where pups and their people can relax, refresh, and refuel.

In Good Paws

At Paws n’ Play, you and your pup will be greeted by a small, knowledgeable team. Our staff members are trained in dog behavior and certified in Pet First Aid, ensuring safety and comfort during your visit. With attentive care and expertise, we’re here to make your time in the café calm, happy, and stress-free.
