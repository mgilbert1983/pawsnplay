Dog Walking Policies & Requirements

Whether it’s a solo stroll or a pack adventure, our professional walkers make every outing engaging, safe, and tail-wagging fun. All our walkers are licensed, insured, bonded, and Pet First Aid certified.

General Requirements A City of Vancouver Dog License is required. Services must be prepaid.
Group Walk Commitment A commitment to at least one walk per week is required for Group Walks.
Cancellations Appointments canceled less than 24 hours before the start time will be billed the full fee.
Walkers & Safety Our staff are licensed, insured, bonded, and trained in Pet First Aid. All walkers use GPS trackers and carry waste removal supplies.
