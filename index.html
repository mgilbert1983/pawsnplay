About Paws n' Play

Welcome to Paws n’ Play — Vancouver’s first comprehensive dog hub, featuring the city’s first licensed Dog Café alongside full-service grooming, walking, and retail. It’s a vibrant community space where pups and their people can relax, refresh, and refuel.
